what does gifting with purpose mean?

Gifting with purpose means the recipient is receiving a thoughtful, gorgeous gift, and the gift giver is doing something great for the organization they support: an excellent way to support the ones you care about most. With skillfully selected product lines, #Fantribution offers gifts that people will actually want!

Our wide variety of giftable products ensure there is something for everyone and every price point! Whether the gift is for a special occasion, or for yourself, #Fantribution will have the perfect gift for any day that ends in "Y".

Why the name fantribution?

#Fantriubtion: the act of uniting YOUR BIGGEST FANS to contribute to your cause by practicing the art of gifting with purpose.
